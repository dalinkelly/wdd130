Washington

Washington is a state in the Pacific North West of the United States. It is bordered by Idaho, Oregon, and our neighboring country Canada. Washington is known for it's beautiful outdoors and heavy presence in the lumber industry. While the state capital is Olympia, Seattle seems to have a stronger tourist presence and is home to many tourist attractions such as the Space Needle, Pikes Place Market, the Gum Wall, and much more.
